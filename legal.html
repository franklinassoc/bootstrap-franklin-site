Ethical Standards
The owners and staff of Franklin Associates are committed to conducting our business in accordance with the law, and with integrity, honesty, and fairness. This applies to our dealings with clients, with suppliers, and with each other. Firm President Perry Franklin is a registered lobbyist with the State of Louisiana and is careful to stay informed of and to follow the laws and guidelines adopted by the State Ethics Board www.ethics.la.gov. Vice President Risa Mueller is certified to provide ethics training to Louisiana public officials. Additionally, as a certified planner, James Taylor is committed to follow the AICP Code of Ethics www.planning.org/ethics/ethicscode and receives continuing education annually.
Equal Employment Opportunity
Franklin Associates is dedicated to both the letter and the spirit of the equal opportunity employment laws. All applicants and all employees will be evaluated on the basis of their ability, competence, and performance of the essential functions of their positions. Franklin does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, sex, age, religion, disability, national origin, citizenship, or status as a disabled Vietnam era veteran, or any other protected basis. Franklin complies with all federal, state and local Equal Opportunity Employment laws, regulations and ordinances. Franklin is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
Our full EEO statement is outlined in our Employee Handbook.
MBE / Hudson Initiative
Franklin Associates is certified by the Southern Region Minority Supplier Development Council as a Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and have secured MBE certifications is several other jurisdictions. Franklin is also a Hudson Initiative firm, certified by the Louisiana Department of Economic Development.
